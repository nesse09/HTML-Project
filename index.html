Type of Publishing

In the past I've really wanted to take the traditional publishing route, but I've started to wonder if maybe I should try to independently publishing on a website such as amazon. There are many benefits to self-publishing such as:

  • You have full control of what to publish in your work
  • You get to keep all of the royalty money
  • It takes less time to self-publish than to go the traditional route

as Zoe Winters once said about self-publishing:

"What ever you may have heard, self-publishing is not a shortcut to anything. Except maybe insanity. Self-publishing, like every other kind of publishing, is hard work. You don't wake up one morning good at it. You have to work for that."

Although self-publishing can be challenging and take a lot of work, it is worth it in the end to get your work published for the world to see.
